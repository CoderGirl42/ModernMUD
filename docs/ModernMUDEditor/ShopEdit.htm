Editing Shops

Back to the index

Shops are an optional part of a zone. A shop is assigned to a specific mob, who is the "shopkeeper".

The main attributes of a shop are the prices when buying/selling an item and the types of items that the shop buys and sells.

Buy Percent: This is the percent of full value that the shopkeeper pays for items and should be less than 100.

Sell Percent: This is the percent of full value that the shopkeeper sells items for and should be at least 100.

Open and Close Hour: If this shop is only open for part of the day, these should be set. By default, shops are open from hour 0 through hour 23, meaning all day long.

Items for Sale: These are specific object indexes that are always available in the shop.

Buys Types: These are the types of objects that the shop will buy.
