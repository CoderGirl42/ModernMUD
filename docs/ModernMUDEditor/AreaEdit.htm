Editing Area Settings

Back to the index

The area settings editor lets you change global area values, such as a the area name and area flags.

Name: The title of the area. this is displayed to players.

Version: This is a version number you can use to identify the revision number of the zone. This is only for your use.

Min and Max Level: These are the level of players this zone is intended for.

Author: This should be your name.

Builders: This will be you and anyone else who has modified or extended the zone.

Reset Msg: This is the message displayed to all players in the zone when it respawns. This can be left blank.

Width and Height: This is only used for worldmap zones. You can ignore this setting.

Recall: This is the default respawn point or "safe room" for the zone.

Justice type: This is the type of justice used in the zone. This has two components -- the alignments allowed in the zone and whether laws are enforced. Justice types inlcuding 'chaotic' do not have laws but still restrict alignments. This should only be used for hometowns.

Judge room: This is the room where the mob presiding over zone justice is located (if laws are enforced in this zone).

Barracks: This is the room where justice guards are spawned when they need to protect the city from invaders or arrest criminals.

Guards: This is the mob that is the justice guard for this room.

Squads: This is the number of squads of justice guards the zone can spawn.

Squad Size: This is the number of guard mobs for each unit of justice guards.

Reset Mode: This is the way the zone respawns.

Reset Time: This is the minimum time between respawns of this zone.

Area Flags: These are area-wide flags and settings that can be set.
